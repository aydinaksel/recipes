# Ingredients

- 2 Liters Water
- 2 Tablespoon Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
- 4 Pounds Potatoes (Russet Potatoes), 2-3 in. Nuggets

# Method

1. Boil Water, add for 10 Minutes
2. Fork should pierce but give some resitance
3. 210 C Roast
4. 20 Minutes, then flip
5. About 45 minutes total
